# GDR Pattern Reader

Turn Geometry Dash macro replays into **easy-to-count patterns** — the same idea as the browser tool, but inside the game.

- Load `.gdr2` replays
- Pick a level section by **in-game percent**
- See burst counts like **1 · 123 · 123** and late/long-hold markers

Pause the level and tap **Pattern** to open the reader.
